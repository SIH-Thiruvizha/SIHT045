Anxiety
Anxiety is a common mental health condition characterized by persistent worry, fear, and nervousness, often accompanied by physical symptoms such as rapid heartbeat and sweating.
Psychotic disorder
Psychotic disorders, like schizophrenia, involve disruptions in thinking, emotions, and perceptions, leading to hallucinations, delusions, and impaired social functioning.
Personality Disorder
Personality refers to enduring patterns of thoughts, feelings, and behaviors that shape individuals' unique traits and characteristics, influencing how they interact with others and navigate the world.
Trauma stress
Trauma and stress-related disorders, such as PTSD, occur in response to traumatic events, leading to symptoms like flashbacks, nightmares, and heightened arousal.
Sleep Disorder
Sleep disorders affect sleep patterns, causing disturbances in duration, quality, or timing of sleep, which can lead to daytime fatigue, irritability, and impaired cognitive function.
Happy
Happiness is a positive emotional state characterized by feelings of joy, contentment, and satisfaction, often influenced by factors such as relationships, achievements, and personal fulfillment.
Eating Disorder
Eating disorders involve disturbances in eating behaviors and attitudes toward body weight and shape, leading to significant physical and psychological consequences.
