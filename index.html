About Us !

Welcome to our Mental Health Assessment website, a platform dedicated to promoting mental well-being and providing support for those in need. Our mission is to raise awareness about mental health issues, offer accessible resources for self-assessment, and share valuable tips for maintaining good mental health.Our Website is for Self-identifying mental health status and gives guidance towards maintaining psychological well-being. However, individuals often face challenges in recognizing their own mental health issues and knowing where to seek help. This can lead to untreated conditions, worsening symptoms, and decreased quality of life.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health is treated with the same importance as physical health. By breaking down stigmas and barriers, we strive to create an inclusive space where everyone feels empowered to prioritize their mental well-being.
