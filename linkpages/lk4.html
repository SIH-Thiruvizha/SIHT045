MINDFULL WALKING....

Mindful walking or hiking involves paying close attention to the sensations of walking or hiking and being present in the moment. It can help reduce stress and anxiety while promoting a sense of calmness and well-being.

Here are some tips for practicing mindful walking or hiking:

  • Start by finding a quiet and peaceful location for your walk or hike.
  • Focus on your breathing and the sensation of your feet touching the ground with each step.
  • Notice the sights, sounds, and smells around you without judgment.
  • Stay present and try to let go of any distractions or worries.
  • Take your time and enjoy the experience of being outdoors.
