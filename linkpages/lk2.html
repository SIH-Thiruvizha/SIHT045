BODY POSITIVE WORKOUT..

Body-positive workouts focus on embracing and celebrating your body as it is, rather than striving for a specific appearance or weight. These workouts promote self-acceptance, confidence, and joy in movement, regardless of size or shape.

Here are some key aspects of body-positive workouts:

  • Focus on how exercise makes you feel rather than how it makes you look.
  • Choose activities that you enjoy and that nourish your body, mind, and spirit.
  • Listen to your body and honor its needs, whether that means pushing yourself or taking a rest.
  • Celebrate your progress and achievements, no matter how small.
