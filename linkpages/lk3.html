Tai chi

Know about Tai chi !

Tai Chi is a traditional Chinese martial art that involves slow, gentle movements and deep breathing. It is often practiced for its numerous health benefits, including relaxation and improved balance.

Here are concise instructions for practicing Tai Chi:

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent.
  2. Relax shoulders and breathe deeply, focusing on the abdomen.
  3. Begin with slow, flowing movements, keeping joints loose.
  4. Maintain balance and rhythm, shifting weight smoothly.
  5. Practice regularly for improved relaxation and balance.
